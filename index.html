DESCRIPTION:
Chem decoder is a free easy to use informative scanner app for the skin-conscious
drugstore product users which allows them to scan the cosmetic ingredients list to
check if its safe for their skin or not and also gives clear insights into what the
ingredients do to the skin with our science-based but easy-to-follow explanations.
TAGLINE:
Scan,decode,analyse the cosmetics at a press of a button.
FUNCTION:
Chem decoder scans,analyses and decodes the ingredients of any cosmetic
product in front your phone camera and it can translate the scientific information
of over 300,000 products and 200,000 ingredients in our database so the user can
easily process and understand it.
